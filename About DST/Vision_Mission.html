Vision And Mission

Vision:

To continuously shape a seamless science, technology and innovation ecosystem, which is cutting-edge, collaborative, inclusive, relevant and aligned to our national aspirations and priorities and build a vision for a Viksit Bharat by 2047.

Mission:

Within the mandates of DST in the creation of S&T infrastructure and manpower, support to basic science research along with S&T led innovation and startups, technology development and seed the cutting-edge, futuristic areas of S&T, by devising suitable programs, schemes and technology missions to address socio-economic challenges and create opportunities to empower its stakeholders.
