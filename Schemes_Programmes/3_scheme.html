Innovation and Technology Development Programmes

Innovation and Technology Development Programmes focus on transforming scientific ideas into viable technologies and products. These programmes encourage entrepreneurship, technology incubation, and start-up ecosystems.

The department supports innovators and enterprises in scaling up technologies for commercial and societal applications.

Key Initiatives

  • Technology incubation and acceleration
  • Support for start-ups and innovators
  • Technology transfer and commercialization
  • Industry-academia collaboration
